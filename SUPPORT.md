# Support

- **Bugs & feature requests** — open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/jovandyaz/cenote/issues) using the templates.
- **Security vulnerabilities** — do not open a public issue; follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
- **Usage & docs** — see the [documentation site](https://jovandyaz.github.io/cenote/).

cenote is maintained by a single author ([@jovandyaz](https://github.com/jovandyaz)); responses are best-effort.
